Welcome to LiftKit, the social lifting app created by two young entrepreneurs, a 16 and 18 year old kid!

We created LiftKit to bring together a community of fitness enthusiasts and provide them with a platform to connect, share workouts and progress, and motivate each other to reach their goals.

At LiftKit, we believe that age is just a number and that anyone can be a successful entrepreneur with a little hard work and determination. As teenagers, we have experienced firsthand the challenges of balancing school, sports, and other extracurricular activities, so we understand the importance of finding ways to fit in a good workout. That's why we designed LiftKit to be user-friendly and accessible to people of all ages and fitness levels.

Whether you're a beginner looking to start your fitness journey or an experienced lifter looking to take your workouts to the next level, LiftKit has something for everyone. With a wide range of features and learnging channels to choose from, you can find the perfect routine to help you achieve your goals. Plus, with our social features, you can connect with other users, share your progress, and get motivated by the LiftKit community.

So why choose LiftKit?

So why choose LiftKit over other fitness apps? For starters, our app was created by two young people who understand the challenges and motivations of their peers. We know what it's like to struggle with finding the motivation to work out, or to feel self-conscious about starting a new fitness routine. That's why we've designed LiftKit to be a supportive and inclusive community, where users can find encouragement and motivation from their peers.

In addition to our community aspect, we also offer a unique feature called "LiftToday" that keeps you accountable to your daily fitness routine by making you take a picture in the same pose once a day. What is the point of this? It keeps you accountable by physically showing you the positive changes you're making on your body.

At LiftKit, we believe that staying active and healthy should be accessible and enjoyable for everyone, regardless of age or fitness level. That's why we've made our app free to download and use, with optional premium features available for those who want to take their training to the next level.

We hope you'll join us on our mission to inspire and support young people on their fitness journey. Thank you for choosing LiftKit, and we can't wait to see you reach your goals!
